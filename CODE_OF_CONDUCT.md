# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Assume good intent while discussing
technical trade-offs, and focus criticism on behavior, code, and evidence rather
than people.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate intimidation, and publishing
someone else's private information are not acceptable.

## Scope

This applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and
other project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately through GitHub's
[private reporting form](https://github.com/m-morino/saikai/security/advisories/new).
Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.

Project maintainers may edit or remove inappropriate contributions and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.
